How It Works

Six steps to a real connection

No swiping. No cold algorithms guessing. Just people who care about you making thoughtful introductions.

👤
01

Create Your Profile

Share your personality, interests, values, and relationship goals — not just photos.

🔵
02

Build Your Circle

Invite trusted friends and family to join your Match Circle — your personal matchmakers.

💌
03

Receive Referrals

When your circle spots a match, they recommend that person with a personalized note explaining why.

🤝
04

Warm Introductions

Both parties see who made the connection and why — creating trust and reducing awkwardness.

💬
05

Double Opt-In Chat

If both people are interested, connect directly or opt for a group intro chat with the mutual connection.

⭐
06

Reputation Layer

Matchmakers earn Trust Badges based on successful connections — making future referrals even more meaningful.
